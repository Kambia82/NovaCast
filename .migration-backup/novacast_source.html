Step 4 of 5 — Optional
Did Anything Happen Recently?
What happened in the last 1–3 days can change where fish are and what they'll bite. Select all that apply, or skip if nothing notable.
🌦️
Light Rain Yesterday
Slight water clarity drop, small pressure changes. Fish are returning to normal but still slightly off. Water may be lightly stained.
⛈️
Heavy Rain in Last 24hrs
Runoff has muddied the water significantly. Fish pushed into flooded shallow areas — newly covered bushes, grass, timber. Big change in behavior.
🌧️
Rained 2–3 Days Ago
Water clarity recovering but still off. Fish settling back to normal patterns. A transitional day — both shallow and mid-depth are worth checking.
🌡️
Big Temperature Drop Recently
Cold front aftermath. Fish went deep, stopped feeding aggressively. Finesse presentations only — don't expect them to chase anything.
☀️
Warm Streak (3+ Sunny Days)
Water has warmed up faster than normal. Spawn may be further along. Fish are more active and have moved shallower than seasonal average.
🏜️
No Rain in a Week+
Water is clearing up and settling. Levels may be dropping slightly. Fish have returned to predictable structure spots. Good clarity = finesse presentations.
